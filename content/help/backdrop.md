# Integrating Counter.dev in Backdrop CMS

Integrating the Counter.dev tracking script in your Backdrop CMS website allows
you to track visitors and gain valuable insights into your website's
performance. Follow the steps below to integrate Counter.dev into your Backdrop
CMS site:

1. Install the Counter module on your site:
   https://backdropcms.org/project/counter

2. Login and enable the Counter module by navigating to Administration >
   Functionality.

3. Navigate to Administration > Configuration > User Accounts > Permissions, and
   grant the 'Administer Counter analytics' permission to whichever roles
   need to configure the module.

4. Navigate to Administration > Configuration > Web Services > Counter, enter
   the tracking ID and UTC offset from your Counter.dev tracking script, then
   select which roles should be tracked.

5. Log in to your Counter.dev account and check the analytics dashboard to see
   if your Backdrop CMS website is being tracked.

Congratulations! You have successfully integrated the Counter.dev tracking
script into your Backdrop CMS website and can now start tracking visitors and
analyzing data to improve your website's performance.
